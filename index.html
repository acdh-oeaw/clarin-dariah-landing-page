CLARIN & DARIAH Joint Initiatives

CLARIN and DARIAH are two European Research Infrastructure Consortia for data-driven research in the Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences. They offer technical and social infrastructures in a sustainable way and also play important roles in educating new generations of researchers and shaping national and European research policy.

CLARIN’s and DARIAH’s members are countries. Member countries implement and run national infrastructures that conform to either one of the two initiatives' requirements. In some countries, national infrastructures conform to both CLARIN’s and DARIAH’s requirements. Consequently CLARIN and DARIAH will check continuously that these requirements are compatible.

Cooperation Projects

CLARIN and DARIAH cooperate in several EU projects, most recently in PARTHENOS, SSHOC, the ERIC Forum and the Digital Humanities Course Registry, and on policy papers.

The Digital Humanities Course Registry, hosted under this domain, is a community-driven platform for a most up-to-date overview of DH courses offered at universities worldwide.
